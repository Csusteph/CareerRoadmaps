Career Movement: A Personalized Journey

Career progression unfolds at its own rhythm. While some employees are eager to explore new roles quickly, others prefer to remain in their current positions, patiently awaiting the right opportunity — sometimes for years. By developing a career roadmap, employees can navigate their journeys more strategically. Staying visible, fostering relationships, and leveraging insights from supervisors and human resources are essential practices that assist employees in identifying their next best move at RCCB.

Internal Mobility

Applying Internally

How to apply for a new role within RCCB.

RCCB is committed to promoting from within. When a new opportunity opens, internal candidates are encouraged to apply — your experience and knowledge of our culture is a real advantage.

1
Talk to your manager

Have a candid conversation with your supervisor about your interest in exploring new roles. This shows initiative and keeps the relationship transparent.

2
Connect with HR

Reach out to your HR Business Partner to discuss your career goals. They can provide guidance on timing, eligibility, and which roles may be a great fit.

3
Search internal job postings

Browse current openings on the Reyes internal job board. Filter by department, location, or level to find your opportunity.

Browse Open Roles →
4
Apply as an internal candidate

Complete the application and make sure to indicate you are an internal candidate. Update your resume to highlight your RCCB experience and any new skills.

5
Prepare for the process

Treat the interview as you would any professional interview. Use the STAR method to share specific examples that demonstrate your competencies and impact.

Remember

You must be in good standing in your current role and have been in your current position for a minimum amount of time before applying. Check with your HR Business Partner for specific eligibility guidelines.
